💡 Safety Tips

1

Stay Aware

Always be aware of your surroundings, especially in unfamiliar areas.

2

Share Your Location

Keep your location sharing enabled with trusted family or friends.

3

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, remove yourself from the situation immediately.

4

Learn Self-Defense

Consider taking self-defense classes to boost your confidence.

5

Keep Emergency Numbers

Save emergency contacts on your phone for quick access.

6

Use Well-Lit Routes

Prefer well-lit, populated areas when walking alone at night.
